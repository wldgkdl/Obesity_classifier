Prediction of top sectors for the year 2006
We have used state of the art pre-trained language model and fine-tuned it on approximately 2,000 news articles. The fine-tuned model provides sentiment scores for each news article.

Using the sentiment scores of news feed data from Jan 2006 to Jun 2006, we are going to predict the top-performing sectors for Jul 2006 - Dec 2006. We compare the recommendation of sentiment score and performance of MSCI past indexes.
We evaluate these recommendations with a future MSCI index and help a customer make a sound investment decision.
